About

This website was created with the purpose of showcasing my logo creations which were developed for both university projects as well as other personal projects.

Although logo creation is a personal hobby of mine, these logos have been and are currently being used for personal projects, businesses and communities. Each logo has its own unique story to tell, so feel free to observe! Note: the polygonal framing is just used for design, and is not part of the logos.

Exhibit A

Lets take a little "expedition" into the story of this logo. As part of a university project, it was required to create an innovative solution for the travel industry during the Covid Pandemic. My group's solution was a virtual reality headset that allows the user to undergo online expeditions at any tourist destination around the world.

This heavily inspired the name of the company to be "Xpedition". In order to fit the company name, I created a logo in the shape of the letter "x". The thick diagonal line in the logo represents the shape of the virtual realtiy headset that my group and I protoyped.

Exhibit B

Prioritask. A word combination of priority and task. This name was created for a future personal project of an automatic timetable/organisation application for students with busy study schedules.

This logo was designed in procreate. I tried to create a logo that is simple, one that matches the theme of organising tasks, as well as representing the name.

Exhibit C

The UTS Business discord is a community with over 1000 students in the UTS business faculty. In 2021, I was the co-owner of this server and helped other students in subjects related to this course.

I created the official logo for this community, which is still in use today. It is a simple, yet sleek design and is representative of UTS through the blue colour scheme.
